Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026 · Effective: June 22, 2026

RiceLayer is a browser extension that restyles the appearance of the webpage you are looking at using CSS only. Our core promise: we change how a website looks, never how it works, and never by reading your private data.

The short version. RiceLayer does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. It does not read your passwords, form inputs, payment details, page text, or private messages. The only thing ever sent off your device is an optional, value-free structural summary of a page — and only at the moment you ask for an AI-generated theme.

1. Who we are

This policy describes how the RiceLayer browser extension and its supporting backend service handle information. If you have any questions, contact us at cimmonster1@gmail.com.

2. What we do and don't access

RiceLayer NEVER

  • Reads passwords
  • Reads form input or field values
  • Reads payment or card information
  • Captures full page text or HTML
  • Reads private messages or DMs
  • Tracks your browsing across sites
  • Sells or rents any data

RiceLayer ONLY

  • Acts on the tab you click it on
  • Analyzes page structure locally
  • Applies a sanitized CSS-only theme
  • Saves your preferences in the browser
  • Sends a value-free page summary only when you request an AI theme

3. Information processed

Processed locally in your browser (never leaves your device on its own)

To restyle a page, RiceLayer reads the page's structure: element counts, detected structural roles (such as navigation, cards, forms), the existing color palette, a typography summary, and layout density. This analysis happens entirely in your browser and is not transmitted unless you explicitly request an AI-generated theme (see below).

Stored locally via chrome.storage

Your preferences — selected presets, per-site theme settings, saved themes, and whether a theme should auto-reapply on a given site — are stored locally in your browser. You can delete them at any time from the extension's Options page or by removing the extension. This data is not sent to us.

Sent to our backend only when you request an AI theme

When (and only when) you ask RiceLayer to generate a theme from a text prompt, it sends a minimized, value-free summary of the current page to our backend so a theme can be generated. Specifically:

  • The site hostname (e.g. example.com)
  • A page-category guess
  • Element counts and detected structural roles
  • An existing color / typography / layout summary
  • Safety flags — whether password, payment, or other sensitive form fields are present (a true/false signal only, never their contents)
  • Your short text prompt (up to 50 characters)
We do not send full HTML, page text, form values, passwords, payment information, or private messages. The structural summary contains no field values and no page content.

4. How information is used

DataPurpose
Local page structureGenerate or apply a CSS-only theme on the current page
Preferences & saved themesRemember your choices so you don't reconfigure each site
Structural summary + promptGenerate an AI theme via our backend; returned as CSS only

We do not use any information for advertising, profiling, creditworthiness, or any purpose unrelated to restyling the page you chose.

5. Third-party services

To produce AI-generated themes, our backend sends the value-free structural summary and your prompt to Google's Gemini API, which returns theme data as strict JSON / CSS only. Your use of this feature is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. The AI provider's API key is read only on our server from its environment and is never exposed to the browser. We do not use any other analytics, advertising, or tracking third parties.

6. Permissions and why they are needed

PermissionWhy
activeTabAct only on the tab you explicitly click the extension on — not across all browsing.
scriptingInject the content script and CSS-only theme layer into that active tab after you activate it.
storageSave your preferences and per-site theme settings locally.
Host permission (backend)Contact our backend API to generate AI themes. Broad host access is optional and requested per-site only when you choose "always apply on this site."

RiceLayer does not load or execute remote JavaScript, WebAssembly, or other remote code. All executable logic ships inside the extension package; the backend returns only CSS theme data, which is sanitized before use.

7. Data retention

Preferences and saved themes remain in your browser until you delete them or remove the extension. Structural summaries sent for AI generation are used to fulfill the request and are not used to build user profiles. We do not maintain user accounts.

8. Security

Page analysis is value-free by design, requests are transmitted over HTTPS, and theme CSS is sanitized at multiple layers (server and extension) before it is ever applied. Sensitive pages such as banking, login, and checkout are treated cautiously and protected by default.

9. Children's privacy

RiceLayer is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

10. Your choices and controls

  • CSS-only transformations with a one-click reset on every page.
  • Per-site enable/disable and a global on/off switch.
  • Delete any saved theme or all stored data from the Options page.
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data.

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
